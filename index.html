About
Our website takes in data such as income, age, and spending on major categories like housing, transportation, food, clothing, luxuries. Then, it runs a regression model. It takes in the data from consumers across the US and provides an output to the consumer on how much their spending should be based on the demographics and income information they provided.
The information provided will a visualization of your spending to help improve your budgeting.
